# Getting High on Climbing

To begin the year 2019 on a high note, Preferred.AI went on a group outing to [Climb Central](https://www.climbcentral.sg/), an indoor rock climbing gym at Kallang Wave Mall. With the exception of a few, this was a new experience to many of us. Nevertheless, no one was daunted, and we were all up for the vertical challenge.
