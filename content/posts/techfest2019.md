# TechFest 2019

**[Preferred.AI](https://preferred.ai/about)** held a TechFest on **Friday 23 August 2019, 3.30pm – 6.30pm** to share the recent projects that we’re actively pursuing.

[

#### Click here to download the brochure for Techfest 2019

](/uploads/2019/08/Brochure.pdf)[Download](/uploads/2019/08/Brochure.pdf)

*   Captivating talks based on recent top AI publications 
*   Useful libraries to build AI/ML-based models/apps
*   Cool demos & apps with novel use cases
*   Information on future opportunities with us

### **Session I – Talks (3.30PM to 5.00PM)**

**SMU School of Information Systems, Seminar Room B1-1**

*   **Preferred.AI:** [Preferences and Recommendations from Data & AI](https://www.dropbox.com/s/az8g0lnz56x7msb/techfest2019-introduction.pdf?dl=0)
    – an overview of our activities and how you can get involved
*   **PCRL:** [Jointly Modeling User Preferences and Learning Deep Item Features from Auxiliary Data](https://www.dropbox.com/s/lt5bb013gpovvu5/techfest2019-pcrl.pdf?dl=0)
    – a publication at [UAI-18](http://auai.org/uai2018/)
*   **MRG:** [Multimodal Review Generation for Recommender Systems](https://www.dropbox.com/s/cv6x2r60ztrws3w/techfest2019-mrg.pdf?dl=0)
    – a publication at [WWW-19](https://www2019.thewebconf.org/)
*   **CompareLDA:** [A Topic Model for Document Comparison](https://www.dropbox.com/s/pj2ynbog5ic7mfi/techfest2019-comparelda.pdf?dl=0)
    – a publication at [AAAI-19](https://aaai.org/Conferences/AAAI-19/)
*   **MP-SimRank:** [Multiperspective Graph-theoretic Similarity Measure](https://www.dropbox.com/s/fwba9mtywnzyrpw/techfest2019-mpsimrank.pdf?dl=0)
    – a publication at [CIKM-18](https://www.cikm2018.units.it/)

Click each title above for the respective slides. Video recordings of the talks are available [here](https://www.youtube.com/playlist?list=PL291RJWFNQGLjPA8WIcVgRmOQGY3P7RzP).

### **Session II – Posters and Demos (5.00PM to 6.30PM)**

**SMU School of Information Systems, Concourse opposite OCBC**

*   **Cerebro**: [Closed-loop recommendation retrieval engine](https://cerebro.preferred.ai)
*   **Cornac**: [Multimodal recommender system library](https://cornac.preferred.ai)
*   **JioApp**: [Recommendations for group meetups](https://jioapp.preferred.ai)
*   **Propedia**: [Web-mined product encyclopedia](https://propedia.preferred.ai)
*   **SentiVec**: [Sentiment-infused word embeddings](https://sentivec.preferred.ai)
*   **ThriftCity**: [Web-sourced price comparisons](https://thriftcity.preferred.ai)
*   **Venom**: [Focused crawler for the deep Web](https://venom.preferred.ai)
*   **Butler**: [Conversational recommender system with natural language explanations](http://t.me/ButlerPreferredAI_Bot)
*   **FaceInMotion**: [Face-based intelligent emotion detection](https://faceinmotion.preferred.ai)
*   **MindReader**: [News recommendation app based on reading history](https://mindreader.preferred.ai)
*   **Neural Network Lab**: [Machine learning in your browser](https://nnlab.preferred.ai)
