# KDD 2023 in Long Beach, California

In August 2023, Hady attended the 2023 ACM SIGKDD International Conference on Knowledge Discovery and Data Mining, also known as KDD 2023. The conference was held at the Long Beach Convention Center.

![KDD 2023 by the numbers](/uploads/2023/08/IMG_9854.jpg)

The conference was run in fully in-person mode. The rich program included 313 research track papers as well as 184 applied data science papers. In addition, there were numerous workshops and tutorials that took place across the five conference days.

![Rich program included yoga in the morning](/uploads/2023/08/IMG_9921.jpg)

It was particularly impressive that in the midst of the academic programmes, the conference also organized yoga in the morning and offered child care to conference attendees with young children.

![Hady at the poster session](/uploads/2023/08/IMG_9879.jpg)

At the conference, Hady presented the paper [Topic Modeling on Document Networks with Dirichlet Optimal Transport Barycenter](https://www.dropbox.com/scl/fi/7i58277jvor6vhdj0x1lv/tkde23barycenter.pdf?rlkey=fabd3nrtfafb15hqr9glqyw7j&dl=0). This was first-authored by [Zhang Ce](https://sites.google.com/view/delvincezhang), also in collaboration with [Rex Ying](https://www.cs.yale.edu/homes/ying-rex/), who will be his future postdoc supervisor at Yale University. The paper itself was presented both orally as well as a poster.

![Hady and Tuan at dinner](/uploads/2023/08/IMG_9895.jpg)

At the conference, Hady also met [Tuan](https://www.qttruong.com/), who is now with Amazon and flew in from Vancouver. Catching up over meals and drinks, we also found the time to enjoy the sun and the beach, as well as cycling over the long stretch of Long Beach.
